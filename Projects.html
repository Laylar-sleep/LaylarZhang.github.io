Mask Detection based on ML

This is a machine learning program to detect face mask wearing with live camera stream.
We applied MobileNet based on Tensorflow.

We can detect 4 kinds of mask wearing situations with around 98% accuracy:

  • with mask
  • incorrectly wear (mouth & nose not covered)
  • incorrectly wear (nose not covered)
  • without mask
